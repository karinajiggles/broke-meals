## What you'll need

A large pot, a knife, and a wooden spoon.

## Instructions

1. Chop the onion and carrots into small pieces.
2. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat and cook the onion and carrots for 5 minutes, until softened.
3. Rinse the red lentils under cold water, then add them to the pot along with the stock cube and 4 cups of water.
4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the lentils fall apart.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Blend with an immersion blender for a smoother soup, if you like.

## Notes

Keeps well in the fridge for 4 days, and freezes nicely too — make a double batch to cut the cost per serving even further.
